About the work

Data only matters when it answers the question a decision-maker is already losing sleep over.

I am a Business Intelligence–focused Data Analyst with hands-on experience transforming operational, financial and compliance data into actionable insights. My work begins with the question, not the dataset, I sit with stakeholders to understand what decision they need to make, then engineer the reporting and dashboards that make that decision obvious.

My strength lies in bridging business needs with data: structuring information from multiple sources, identifying trends that move the bottom line, and presenting findings in a way that drives performance improvement at every level, from production floor to executive boardroom.

Alongside this practice, I am currently pursuing an MBA in Corporate Management , set to complete at the end of 2026 , sharpening the strategic, governance and financial-management lens I bring to every reporting engagement. The analytics gets you to the right answer; the MBA training keeps that answer commercially defensible in the room where the decision is made.
